Frequently Asked Questions

Do you charge by the hour?

No! We quote by volume — you pay for the space your items take in the truck, not by the time it takes.

Is the estimate really free?

Absolutely. We provide free, no-obligation on-site estimates. The price we quote is the price you pay.

What if I have more items than expected?

We'll reassess on-site and adjust the price transparently. You approve before we load anything.

Do you donate usable items?

Yes! We partner with local charities to donate furniture, appliances, and household goods in good condition.
